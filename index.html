OUR MISSION

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity. Indian Youth for Sustainable Development is a NGO who spread awareness about Sustainable Development Goals of United Nations among youth in India.
